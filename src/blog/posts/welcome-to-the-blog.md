Timing beats volume — and that's not just our product pitch, it's the whole reason this blog exists.

Most B2B sales content tells you to work harder: more calls, more lists, more volume. We think that's backwards. This blog is where we'll break down the signals that actually predict a buying window — expansions, funding rounds, leadership changes, RFPs — and how to act on them before your competitors even know the window opened.

Expect practical breakdowns, not fluff.
